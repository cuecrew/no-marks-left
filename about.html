the origin

we asked: what if your
bookmark was a reward?

It started with a simple observation: finishing a book is a big deal. You lived in that world for days, sometimes weeks. You stayed up past midnight for characters who aren't real. You had genuine feelings about things that never happened.

And then it ends. You close the book. And there's nothing to mark the moment — just an empty mug and that particular emptiness that only book-endings bring.

We wanted to change that. So we made a bookmark you can eat when you're done.

"The bookmark that travels with you — and rewards you for finishing."

No Marks Left means two things. No dog-ears, no sticky residue, no grease stains — your pages stay exactly as you'd want them. And no marks left in your reading life either: every book finished, every chapter earned, every story properly closed.

Your bookmark shouldn't just hold a page. It should hold the moment.

the craft

made with obsessive
attention.

We're a small team of readers who also care deeply about chocolate. Every batch is made fresh per order, in a clean food-grade kitchen, using ingredients we'd give to people we actually like.

🍫
Real couverture chocolate
Not compound. Not "chocolate-flavoured". Actual couverture — 70% single-origin dark, ethically-sourced milk, and Belgian white. You'll taste the difference.
🎨
Food-safe edible ink
Designs are printed with food-grade edible ink on edible wafer paper — the same technology professional patisseries use. Every detail is safe to eat.
📦
Made fresh per order
No sitting in warehouses. Every order is made after you place it — ships in 3–5 days in temperature-controlled packaging so it reaches you perfect.
🌿
No artificial preservatives
We don't add shelf-extenders. Our bookmarks have a natural 30-day shelf life. We figure you'll finish the book well before that.
📄
Zero marks on your pages. Guaranteed.
Our bookmarks are finished with a sealed food-grade coating that prevents any oil or cocoa butter transfer onto paper. Sit it between your pages for 30 days — they'll come out clean. Your book is safe. That's a promise, and also the whole point of our name.
FSSAI Certified
Licensed under FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India). Our kitchen and manufacturing processes meet all food safety standards for production and packaging of food products in India. Licence No. will be printed on all packaging.
